Project Overview

SmartCourse is an intelligent recommendation engine designed to make educational discovery seamless and personalized. It leverages cutting-edge Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning to provide users with accurate, context-aware course suggestions. Ask in natural language what you want to learn, and our AI models will find the best courses for you.

System Capabilities

  • Dual recommendation models: TF-IDF & Neural embeddings
  • Dynamic top-10 course suggestions based on your query
  • Relevance scoring with visual progress bars
  • Save preferred courses to your dashboard
  • View complete search history with timestamps
  • Side-by-side model comparison for identical queries

How SmartCourse Works

1. Search Courses

Enter what you want to learn in natural language, e.g., "Python for Data Science".

2. Get Recommendations

Choose between TF-IDF or Neural model to get top-10 dynamic course suggestions with relevance scores.

3. Save & Track

Save your preferred courses and track your learning history on your personalized dashboard.
